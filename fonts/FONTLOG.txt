Modification:
  Instantiated the variable font at wght=400 and subset it with HarfBuzz
  hb-subset 14.2.1. The subset retains modern Hangul syllables, Hangul jamo
  and extended jamo, and common Latin/punctuation/symbol ranges. Large CJK
  ideograph ranges were removed to reduce startup and export memory on iPad.
  Hinting was removed. The resulting static TrueType font remains licensed
  under SIL OFL 1.1. The modified font's primary name is
  "Noto Sans CJK KR"; it does not use the Reserved Font Name "Source".

Metadata note:
  HarfBuzz instantiates the outlines and OS/2 weight at 400 but preserves the
  variable source's legacy "Thin" name-table entry. PDF export supplies the
  explicit BaseFont name NotoSansCJKkr-Regular; glyph shape and text mapping
  are Regular-weight and are covered by the rendering smoke test.
